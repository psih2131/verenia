At Verenia, we believe in the power of conversation to drive technological breakthroughs. Our cutting-edge solutions are shaping the future, from transforming how autonomous vehicles operate to revolutionizing employee engagement.

Verenia started as a company specializing in CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote) software, making a name for itself by simplifying complex sales processes. Our journey took a big leap forward in 2022 when Oracle acquired our NetSuite CPQ solution. This milestone marked a new era for us, inspiring further innovation.

A market leader for more than a decade ,we now offer a products that significantly improve your team efficiency.
